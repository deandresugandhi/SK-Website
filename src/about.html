UNLEASHING THE TRUE

ARTIST

WITHIN YOU

We are SK Music & Production, a Sydney-based music and video production company. Our mission lies in bringing out the artists within our clients and breathe life into their visions. Since our inception in 2018, our team of music and film buffs have produced a diverse range of visual and sonic wonders, from original music arrangements, video covers, motion-graphics, product shoots, to music videos.
